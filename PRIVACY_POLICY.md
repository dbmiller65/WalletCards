# Privacy Policy

Wallet Cards & Photos does not collect, store, or share personal data on external servers.

## Data You Provide

The app allows you to save images of cards and personal photos. These images and any names you assign are stored locally on your device.

## Data Collection

We do not collect data from this app. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Permissions

The app may request access to:

- Camera (to capture card and photo images)
- Photo Library (to select existing images)

These permissions are used only to perform the requested action. Content is stored locally on your device.

## Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties.

## Contact

For support or privacy questions, please open an issue:

https://github.com/dbmiller65/WalletCards/issues

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update it in the repository and in App Store Connect as needed.
